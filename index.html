About Us

NEOTERIC Solutions was established by highly experienced and qualified entrepreneurs with consultancy experiences in wide array of professional and business fields. NEOTERIC Solutions was established in reaction to the developing market demand for high quality innovative products and services at affordable prices, keeping up-to-date with the emerging tastes of the Pakistani market.

Our Mission

Neoteric Solutions aims to provide quality services to the organizations in an innovative manner to ensure the efficiency, adequacy and consistency of their operations to achieve their organizational goals.

Our Vision

NEOTERIC Solutions specialize in the provision of cost effective products and services ranging from electronics products and accessories, supply of food stuffs, beverages, office automations, event management and import and export. NEOTERIC Solutions aim is to provide our customers with efficient, cost- effective, value-for-money customized, specialized and/or general standard products and services; so as to simplify the operations of the customers. NEOTERIC Solutions believes in building the long-term sustainable relationship with its partners

Quality Assurance

We ensure that the qualities of our products are maximized by preserving a comprehensive approved vendor list as part of our Quality Management System. With regularly reviewing both the quality of the products we supply and the performance of our vendors, to ensure that we maintain the standard of goods and services we aim to provide our customers. Similarly, we constantly monitor the market for improved or superior quality goods within our scope of supply and try to enlist these vendors on our panel.
